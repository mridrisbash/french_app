Privacy Policy — French Basics App

Last updated: September 9, 2025

Summary: French Basics App is an offline learning app. We do not collect, store, or share personal data on our servers. Lesson progress is saved locally on your device using standard on-device storage.

1. Who we are

App: French Basics App
Contact email: jafarusmankiri@gmail.com

2. What data we collect

We designed the app to work entirely offline. We do not collect any personal information. Specifically:

  • No account creation and no sign-in.
  • No analytics, telemetry, or crash reporting is sent to us.
  • No location, contacts, photos, microphone, or camera access.

3. On-device data (local only)

The app stores limited learning data only on your device to make the app function offline:

  • Lesson progress & completion status (e.g., which lessons you’ve finished and tests you’ve passed).

This data is kept locally using the platform’s secure preferences storage. We do not have access to it.

4. How we use information

Local data is used solely to:

  • Remember your progress between sessions, and
  • Unlock lessons/tests you’ve completed.

5. Data sharing and transfers

We do not sell, rent, or share your data with third parties. Because the app is offline and does not transmit data to our servers, no data is transferred outside your device.

6. Your choices & controls

  • Reset progress: You can clear the app’s storage via your device settings to reset progress.
  • Uninstall: Removing the app deletes all locally stored data related to the app.

7. Children’s privacy

This app may be used by learners of all ages. We do not collect personal information from anyone, including children. Parents and guardians should supervise device use as they deem appropriate.

8. Security

Your data remains on your device. We recommend keeping your device’s operating system up to date and using a passcode or biometrics to protect access.

9. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect improvements or legal requirements. If we make material changes, we will update the “Last updated” date above. Continued use of the app after changes take effect constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or the app’s privacy practices, contact us at: jafarusmankiri@gmail.com.
